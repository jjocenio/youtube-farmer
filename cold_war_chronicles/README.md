# Channel: Cold War Chronicles
**Niche:** Detailed historical analysis of the geopolitical tension between the USSR and the USA (1947–1991).

## 1. Visual & Narrative Identity
* **Tone:** Analytical, suspenseful, and objective. Think "Espionage Thriller meets Academic Documentary."
* **Narrative Style:** Second-person ("Imagine you are in Berlin, 1961...") mixed with high-level strategic oversight.
* **Pacing:** Fast-paced with frequent visual transitions every 5-8 seconds.

## 2. Scripting Guidelines (for manifest.json)
* **Hook:** Start with a high-stakes moment or a "what if" scenario.
* **Structure:** Introduction -> The Tension -> The Turning Point -> Legacy/Impact.
* **Technical Language:** Use period-accurate terminology (e.g., Brinkmanship, Proxy Wars, MAD, Signal Intelligence).
* **Image Prompts (Fal.ai):** Prompts should specify "Kodachrome 60s film grain," "Cinematic noir lighting," or "Historical archival photo style" to maintain a consistent visual look across videos.

## 3. Recurring Themes
* The Space Race.
* The Iron Curtain and Berlin.
* Proxy wars (Korea, Vietnam, Angola).
* Technological advancements and Nuclear strategy.
* Intelligence and Espionage (CIA vs. KGB).

## 4. Target Audience
* History enthusiasts, "Map Men" style viewers, and students of geopolitics.

## 5. Script Constraints
* **Average Length:** 8-12 minutes (approx. 1500-2000 words).
* **Voice Over:** Deep, resonant male or female voice with a moderate pace.
* **Language:** English (US).
