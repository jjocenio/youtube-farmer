# The UK's Secret Dirty Tricks: Britain's Cold War Black Propaganda Machine

This video exposes the British government's covert Cold War program of so-called 'black propaganda' — roughly 350 classified operations in which MI6 and the Information Research Department forged documents, created fake dissident organisations, and planted disinformation deep inside the Soviet bloc, a campaign that ran for over two decades and remained almost entirely unknown until recent declassification.

The core tension is that while the world focused on CIA covert action, Britain was running its own shadow war of forgeries and front groups — often more sophisticated, and directed not just at Moscow but at neutral and allied nations to shape global opinion during the most dangerous years of the Cold War.

Viewers will walk away understanding that the Cold War was never just a two-superpower game — Britain played a ruthless, calculated third hand that helped define the information battlefield we still live in today.
