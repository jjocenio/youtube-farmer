# The Secret Nuclear City Under Greenland

This video investigates Project Iceworm, the classified Cold War plan to hide a mobile network of U.S. nuclear missile launchers beneath Greenland's ice sheet. Beneath the public story of Camp Century as a scientific Arctic base, the deeper tension is a secret doomsday architecture built on shifting ice, Danish ambiguity, and the logic of survivable retaliation. Viewers will walk away understanding how far nuclear planners were willing to go to make deterrence invisible, mobile, and almost impossible to destroy.
