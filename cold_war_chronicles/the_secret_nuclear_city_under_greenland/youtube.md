## Thumbnail Strategy

### Visual Concept
The thumbnail should center on the illuminated Camp Century tunnel from the hook zone: a frozen corridor under Greenland, lined with military structures and disappearing into darkness. This scene stops the scroll because it creates an immediate contradiction: a city-like installation where no city should exist, buried inside an ice sheet. The emotional hook is secrecy and claustrophobia: the viewer should feel they are looking into a classified place they were never meant to see. It pairs naturally with Cold War Chronicles’ Kodachrome 60s film grain, cinematic noir lighting, and historical archival photo style.
